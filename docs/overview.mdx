## Digital-FTE in one glance

**Digital-FTE** is an open-source autonomous AI employee that watches your communication channels and back-office tools, drafts actions, and executes only after you approve.

- **24/7 Digital Employee** for Gmail, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Odoo, and social media.
- **Dual-Agent Architecture**: a cloud "Sentry" drafts actions; a local "Executive" executes them.
- **Human-in-the-Loop** by design – no action is taken without explicit approval.
- **Self-Evolution Engine** that captures failures and proposes code patches.

If you want a robust, auditable AI employee instead of opaque automation scripts, Digital-FTE is designed for you.
